Noodle Pot

was originally established in 2011 as an authentic Taiwanese restaurant. We specialize in our famous Taiwanese Beef Noodle Soup. Our establishment, as well as many of the others in Chinatown Plaza, has a Journey to the West theme. In our 9 years of establishment, we aim to provide the best food and service to our locals and tourists.

Beef Broth & Tendon Noodles

This is hands down the best Taiwanese place I've ever tried. The noodles were soft and chewy (tasted very much like handmade noodles) , the broth was super flavorful, and there was plenty of green onions, veggies, and meat. The meat was very tender as well. The chives and pork dumplings were very very JUICY so be careful not to squirt the person next to you . As for service, the young lady was super nice and polite. Food came out fast. We went there 2 times in a row and wish we had discovered this place sooner. I would make another trip to Vegas just for this place, seriously.

Review by Susie W. on 5/2/2019
